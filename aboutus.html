About Life Line

Life Line Community Ambulance is ran by volunteers who devote their free time to making a difference in the community.

With a wide range of medical & non-medical backgrounds we assist vulnerable people within the community by taking them to and from their health appointments when other methods are not available, reducing the strain on the North West Ambulance Service.

We source equipment such as wheelchairs and defibrillators that we service and maintain, allowing us to donate them into the wider community.

All of our staff are trained at a minimum level to Emergency First Aid at Work, allowing us to provide an event cover service for all public and private events.
